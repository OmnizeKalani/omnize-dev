Is Omnize Secure?

It's hard to turn on the news today without seeing another company was hacked and their data was leaked... Omnize was built in a time where online platforms are constinitly breached. We have turned to the security experts at one of the biggest cloud providers in the nation so that we could offer unparaled security for you and your customers data!

How long is the onboarding process?

Onboarding is based on the size of the company, it's current e-commerce activities, and how well the data is formated. Ultimately, how fast we can onboard is up to you, the customer! You will be assigned an account manager who will serve as your personal represenative to the development team. So long as you're providing the information requested in a timely manner, onboarding should be completed within a month. (This does not include, additional time for customer development)

I need an integration Omnize doesn't have listed?

Yikes! We don't offer an integration you need? No biggie, give your account manager the details of the integration, some information on who we can contact to get some documentation, and your account manager will give that over to our development team. So long as the endpoint you're wanting to connect will allow us, we will build a custom integration to work inside the Omnize platform. Remember this is a partnership, if you don't succeed neither do we...

How does Omnize differ from its competitors?

That's an easy one! Omnize was built and founded on the principal that any relationship we develop through companies using our platform is to be treated as a partnership. Our account managers will routinely monitor your account and performance. When our account managers see an area where we could increase profitability for you, they will reach out to discuss with your represenative. Our account managers are incentivized by increasing "Your" profit not ours!
